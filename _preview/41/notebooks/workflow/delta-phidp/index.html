Total Differential Phase Shift

Total Differential Phase Shift¶

This notebook focuses on the estimation of the total differential phase shift, ΔΦDPtot\Delta \Phi_{DP}^{tot}ΔΦDPtot​, from polarimetric radar observations.

The estimated ΔΦDPtot\Delta \Phi_{DP}^{tot}ΔΦDPtot​ is an important intermediate quantity in several radar processing tasks, including attenuation correction.

The algorithm is described in detail in Testud et al. (2000), Ryzhkov et al. (2014) and Diederich et al. (2015).

Get lowest elevation sweep¶

First we get the lowest sweep and do some georeferencing.

See Also

  • wradlib.georef.polar.georeference

  • wradlib.georef.projection.get_earth_projection

swp = (
    dtree[f"{root}/sweep_0"]
    .to_dataset()
    .assign_coords(dtree[root].coords)
    .assign_coords(sweep_mode="azimuth_surveillance")
    .wrl.georef.georeference(crs=wrl.georef.get_earth_projection())
).sel(vcp_time="2014")
swp.x.attrs = xd.model.get_longitude_attrs()
swp.y.attrs = xd.model.get_latitude_attrs()
swp.z.attrs = xd.model.get_altitude_attrs()
display(swp)

Mask with RHOHV¶

First, we mask with ρHV\rho_{HV}ρHV​ and/or any other moment you trust to remove unwanted signal.

mask = (swp.RHOHV >= 0.9)
mswp = swp.where(mask)
phimask = mswp.uPhiDP

Estimate ΔΦDPtot\Delta \Phi_{DP}^{tot}ΔΦDPtot​¶

Second, we estimate total differential phase shift ΔΦDPtot\Delta \Phi_{DP}^{tot}ΔΦDPtot​. The range window can be selected according to your data.

dphi = phimask.wrl.dp.delta_phidp(5000.)
display(dphi)
